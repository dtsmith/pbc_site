About Us

We're a Southern Baptist congregation devoted to living out the Great Commission and the Great Commandment as a healthy expression of the Body of Christ. The Lord placed in the hearts of eight Christians the desire to plant a church in a new area of growth in northern Harrison County, Mississippi.

If you would like to know more about Parkway or have any questions about our ministry, please feel free to contact us.

Dr. Argile Smith, Pastor

Mission

Parkway Baptist Church exists to reach around the corner and around the world and share the Good News of God’s love through Jesus Christ.

Values

We value God’s love to us through Jesus Christ and the opportunities He gives us to serve Him by loving people.

Statement of Faith

We believe that ...

  • You can count on the Bible to be totally true and trustworthy
  • God is alive and at work in the world He created
  • God is your Heavenly Father who loves you and wants to save you
  • Jesus Christ is God, and He alone can save you
  • God’s Spirit nudges you toward Christ and lives in you once you are saved
  • You are saved by repenting of your sin and placing your faith in Christ
  • You can grow in your walk with Christ and serve Him with joy
  • The church consists of spiritual siblings in Christ who serve Him together
  • Heaven is the eternal home for people who have given themselves to Christ
  • Christ will come again one day
